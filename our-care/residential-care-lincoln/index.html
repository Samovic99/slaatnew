what is residential care?

Residential care at Monson Care Home is for those who may need some assistance with day to day tasks.

It might be that you just want the peace of mind and security that comes from knowing that the care is available to you 24/7.  You may need assistance and support with mobility, washing, dressing or eating.  You may also have particular needs because of dementia.

Whatever your requirements, your care and support will be person-centred, tailored to your individual needs and delivered by our dedicated and fully trained staff teams.

who is residential care for?

Our care home in Lincoln provides a safe, supportive and friendly environment for those who need a little extra help.

If you or a loved one are finding it difficult to live independently, then residential care may be suitable for you.  At Monson, our focus is on giving our residents independence back, but with the security of knowing our experienced team are on hand 24/7, whenever needed.

Whether it is support with day to day tasks, helping on trips outside or even making new friends with other residents, each individual is different and we provide a range of services to help meet these needs.

what are the benefits of moving to monson for residential care?

At Monson Care Home, we promote independence and encourage residents to stay active, giving them the support they need to live the life they want.

  • 24/7 support from an experienced and compassionate team;
  • Independence and freedom of choice - we are here to facilitate as best we can what you want to do and to help you live life as you want it
  • Extensive activities to keep residents mentally and physically stimulated. Access to gardens & outdoor space for gardening & gentle exercise, or support with trips out
  • Health & Well Being - healthy, home-cooked meals. We strive to ensure every individual receives a healthy, nutritious and well-balanced diet.  All our meals are cooked fresh, on-site by our team of chefs and tailored to personal preferences and dietary requirements
