life in our home

Staying healthy at Monson

More than just a place to live, we offer a home tailored for the comfort and entertainment of individuals and couples in Lincoln.

stimulating activities

Entertainment and activities for everyone

We have an ever-changing range of activities which reflects the interests and capabilities of our residents.

Our in-house activities co-ordinator ensures there is always something interesting and engaging for our residents to get involved in, whether it’s art clubs, exercise classes or a spot of gardening. We also offer trips out to explore the local area.

amazing facilities

Purpose-built home with a range of facilities to suit your every need. Each bedroom at Monson is spacious, fully equipped and has been tastefully decorated.

Events and Activities in our home and in Lincoln

  • All bedrooms have en-suite facilities and High-speed internet so you can stay connected with loved ones. If you prefer something more relaxing, we have a specialist bath with a powered seat transfer
  • Socialise with friends We have a number of lounges, perfect for socializing with friends, over a game of cards, a board game, or simply to have a chat
  • Our air-conditioned lounges have smart TVs and views out to the garden and for those who want some quiet time, we have a library where you can sit back and read a book
  • Your privacy is important to us, that’s why we have a dedicated clinical room that facilitates our attending doctor, district nurses, chiropodist, dentist and optician.

food & nutrition

At Monson, we place great emphasis on providing our residents with fresh and nutritious food.

The home sources quality ingredients which are lovingly prepared by our in-house team each day.

There is a varied and ever-changing menu, from salads and something light in spring/summer to hearty, comforting meals in winter. We strive to accommodate any special dietary requirements, or any particular likes or dislikes.

Drinks, tea and snacks (including delicious, home-baked cakes) are served throughout the day and are available on request.

Meals are served in our dining room, where full table service is provided; for those who want to dine in the peace & quiet of their own room, room service is always available.

Relatives are always welcome to dine with their loved ones; just let us know you’re coming and our staff team will be happy to accommodate you.
