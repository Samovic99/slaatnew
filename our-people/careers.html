Care Assistant - Nights

Care Assistant (Nights) required to join our dedicated and hard working team at Sycamore Lodge Care Home, 2 Burringham Road, Scunthorpe, DN17 2BB

We pride ourselves on providing a safe, clean and comfortable home-from-home environment where we care respectfully and effectively for each resident like a dearly-loved member of our own family.

You will be required to:

  • Provide personal care and attention for each resident, and follow the activities of daily living in accordance with the planned care
  • Work in a person-centred way, ensuring residents retain their dignity and individuality
  • Be involved in the general activities of the home
  • Maintain a safe and secure environment for all residents, staff members and visitors

Skills, Knowledge and Attitudes needed:

  • A friendly, approachable, caring and helpful nature
  • A genuine commitment to providing our residents with the very highest standards of care
  • An ability to communicate effectively when speaking or writing, capable with numbers, and comfortable when using a computer
  • Motivation to work both individually and as part of a team
  • Willingness to complete all necessary training (Moving & Handling, Health & Safety, Safeguarding etc)
  • An ability to show Compassion, Empathy & Commitment to all within the home

Desirable: NVQ Level 2 qualification - or working towards this qualification would be desirable. However if your dedication and passion for the role is there, we will provide you with all necessary training and the chance to complete the NVQ if you join the team and successfully complete our probation period.

Hours: 12 hour shifts working 8pm-8am

Salary: £9.00 per hour - Full or Part time positions available

Domestic Assistant

Domestic Assistant required to join our dedicated and hard working team at Sycamore Lodge Care Home, 2 Burringham Road, Scunthorpe, DN17 2BB

Duties will include, but are not limited to:

  • Maintain the cleanliness and general tidiness of the home's communal areas, offices and residents' rooms and bathrooms
  • Ensure cleaning equipment is used and maintained correctly, and is stored in a safe and clean manner
  • Treat residents with dignity, respect their privacy and ensure their rights are protected at all times
  • Work effectively as part of our dedicated staff team
  • Ensure a safe environment is maintained within the home at all times for residents, staff and visitors

Skills, Knowledge and Attitudes needed:

  • Follow all instructions and established procedures
  • Take pride in your work and pay attention to detail
  • Be able to work unsupervised as part of an effective staff team
  • Be prepared to undertake future training as required to maintain consistently high standards

Hours: Shift system - 8am-2pm and 10am-2pm

Salary: £9 per hour

Housekeeper

Housekeeper required to join our dedicated and hard working team at Sycamore Lodge Care Home, 2 Burringham Road, Scunthorpe, DN17 2BB

Duties will include, but are not limited to:

  • Organise, implement and supervise the cleaning and laundry services in the home in accordance with the agreed standards
  • Evaluate the standards of cleaning to ensure the highest standards are maintained at all times
  • Solve day to day problems which arise

  • Show Compassion, Empathy & Commitment to all within the home

Hours: 32 hours per week with a rolling rota of shifts 10am-4pm, 7am-2pm, 8am-2pm

Salary: £9 per hour
