Care Assistant

Care Assistant required to join our dedicated and hard working team at Deen Care Home, Holywell, Wales

We pride ourselves on providing a safe, clean and comfortable home-from-home environment where we care respectfully and effectively for each resident like a dearly-loved member of our own family.

You will be required to:

  • Provide personal care and attention for each resident, and follow the activities of daily living in accordance with the planned care
  • Work in a person-centred way, ensuring residents retain their dignity and individuality
  • Be involved in the general activities of the home
  • Maintain a safe and secure environment for all residents, staff members and visitors

Skills, Knowledge and Attitudes needed:

  • A friendly, approachable, caring and helpful nature
  • A genuine commitment to providing our residents with the very highest standards of care
  • An ability to communicate effectively when speaking or writing, capable with numbers, and comfortable when using a computer
  • Motivation to work both individually and as part of a team
  • Willingness to complete all necessary training (Moving & Handling, Health & Safety, Safeguarding etc)
  • An ability to show Compassion, Empathy & Commitment to all within the home

Benefits

  • Competitive salary.
  • Generous annual leave.
  • Pension scheme.
  • Ongoing training and development.
  • Employee Assistance Programme.
  • Staff meals during shifts.
  • Uniform provided.
  • Flexible working hours.
  • Staff recognition and reward schemes.
  • Opportunities for career progression.

Desirable: NVQ Level 2 qualification - or working towards this qualification would be desirable. However if your dedication and passion for the role is there, we will provide you with all necessary training and the chance to complete the NVQ if you join the team and successfully complete our probation period.

Hours: 12 hour shifts working 8pm-8am

Salary: £15.00 per hour - Full or Part time positions available

Registered Nurse

Nurse (Full-Time)required to join our dedicated and hardworking team at Deen Care Home.
At Deen Care Home, we take pride in providing a safe, clean, and comfortable home-from-home environment where we care for each resident with the same respect and compassion as a beloved family member. We are seeking competent, experienced nurses who are eager to develop their careers in a nursing home environment. We provide an excellent in-house training program and offer fantastic opportunities for career progression to senior levels.

Duties will include, but are not limited to:

  • Provide high-quality, person-centred care to residents, ensuring their needs are met according to care plans.
  • Administer medications and treatments as prescribed by the medical team.
  • Monitor and record the health status of residents, reporting any changes or concerns to senior staff.
  • Assist in the development and implementation of individual care plans.
  • Ensure that all care documentation is accurate and up to date.
  • Maintain a safe and clean environment for both residents and staff.
  • Engage with residents and families to ensure a high standard of communication and satisfaction.

Skills, Knowledge and Attitudes needed:

  • Registered Nurse (RN) with a valid nursing license.
  • Strong understanding of clinical practices and the ability to deliver high-quality care.
  • Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Ability to work both independently and as part of a team.
  • Compassionate and empathetic approach to care, with a genuine desire to make a difference in residents' lives.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and prioritize effectively.
  • Strong attention to detail and excellent organizational skills.
  • Willingness to complete ongoing training and development to enhance skills and qualifications.
  • Experience in a nursing home or similar healthcare setting is desirable but not essential.

Benefits

  • Generous annual leave allowance.
  • Retail and leisure savings package worth £1,200 per year.
  • Attractive pension scheme.
  • Free life insurance cover.
  • Nurse indemnity insurance cover.
  • Training and progression opportunities.
  • Access to Employee Assistance Programme.
  • Staff introductory bonus scheme.
  • Employee of the month bonus scheme.

Hours: 32 - 48 hours per week, full-time

Salary: £20.61 – £22.03 per hour
