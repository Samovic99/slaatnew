FAQs

General FAQs

What is included in my weekly fee?
All of your care and nursing costs are included. The weekly fee also includes all of your meals, drinks, snacks (including some alcoholic drinks – if required). Physiotherapy sessions are included in line with levels agreed prior to your admission. Additional physiotherapy or Occupational Therapy sessions can always be added at an additional cost.

If you require incontinence aids – these are included in your fees.

Your participation in any of our activities sessions is also included – unless there is a specified admission charge to external events or unless you require an escort to help you attend such events.
What personal belongs can I bring?
You are welcome to bring personal items such as photographs, small furniture pieces, decorative items, and personal care items to make your room feel more like home.
Can my family and friends visit?
Yes, we encourage family and friends to visit. We have flexible visiting hours and various comfortable spaces where you can spend time with your loved ones.
How do I arrange a visit to view the Centre?
You can arrange a visit by contacting our admissions team through our contact form or by calling us directly. We'll be happy to schedule a tour and answer any questions you may have.
Can you accommodate my dietary requirements?
Yes, we cater to various dietary requirements including medical, cultural, and personal preferences. Our kitchen staff works closely with our nutrition team to ensure all needs are met.
What meals are provided?
We provide three main meals daily - breakfast, lunch, and dinner - along with morning tea, afternoon tea, and supper. Snacks are available throughout the day.
What is provided for in the room?
Each room comes fully furnished with a bed, wardrobe, chair, bedside table, and en-suite bathroom. You're welcome to add personal touches to make it feel more like home.
Are there lounges or areas for residents to socialise in?
Yes, we have multiple communal areas including lounges, dining rooms, activity rooms, and outdoor gardens where residents can socialize and participate in activities.
Are there activities for residents to participate in?
Yes, we offer a wide range of daily activities including exercise classes, arts and crafts, music therapy, games, and special events. Our activities program is designed to promote social interaction and well-being.
Do you offer physiotherapy?
Yes, we have qualified physiotherapists who provide both individual and group sessions to help maintain mobility and independence.
Do you have an on-site hairdressing service?
Yes, we have a dedicated salon on-site and regular visits from professional hairdressers who provide a full range of services.

Career FAQ

Who can be employed at Deen Care Homes?
Deen Care Homes welcomes applications from both international candidates and local applicants residing in the UK. We offer sponsorship opportunities for eligible international candidates, including those seeking a visa switch. This means we can support qualified applicants who are already in the UK and wish to upgrade their visa status to work in our care home.
